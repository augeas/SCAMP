3.5.2 Two-Dimensional Seeds

3.5.2.1 Single Defects

This produces a two-dimensional array of 1s on a background of 0s. "xnumber" and "ynumber" set the numbers of columns and rows, "xsep" and "ysep" give the distances between them, which are maximized for a value of 0.

3.5.2.2 Tabula Rasa

This just leaves an array of 0s, which is most usefull for the "Langton's Ant" and "Turmites" automaton types.

3.5.2.3 Random

"Probability" sets the chance of each being initially set to state 1 out of 1000.
