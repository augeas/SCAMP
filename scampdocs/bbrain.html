6.5 Brian's Brain

Brian's Brain is a three-state totalistic cellular automaton introduced by Brian Silverman [Resnick96]. The three states are "live" (white), "dying" (red) and "dead" (black). A dead cell becomes live if two of its eight neighbours are live. Live cells become dying and dying become dead. The resulting behaviour is highly dynamic, with a variety of patterns moving across the grid. There is only one parameter, "occupancy" gives the chance of a cell being initially live out of 1000.
